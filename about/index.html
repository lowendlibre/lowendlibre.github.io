Who are we?⌗

This project is headed by justsoup and hexaheximal. Our main goal is to bring the mobile Linux ecosystem to low-end and generally MediaTek based devices. lowendlibre was founded on July 10, 2023 when our two separate projects merged with the same goal in mind. Linux.
